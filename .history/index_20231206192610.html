About Tedx

WHAT IS TEDx

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxMEC, where x = independently organized TED event. At our TEDxMEC event, TED Talks video and live speakers will combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group.

x = independently organized event

TEDxMEC stands for the mission of TED - to propagate ideas potent enough to change the mindsets, inspire youth at large and equip them to crave a better living, beneficial for themselves and the society they dwell in.TEDxMEC features engaging talks inspiring people ranging from veteran performers and social reformers to young innovators. It serves as a stage for promoting fresh thoughts and inspiring innovation.

Frequently asked questions.

What is TEDx?

TEDx is an independently organized event licensed by TED, where local communities host live speakers to share ideas, experiences, and perspectives.

How can I attend a TEDx event?

To attend a TEDx event, check the official TEDx website or the specific event's page for details on registration, dates, and ticket information.

Are TEDx talks available online?

Yes, most TEDx talks are recorded and made available on the TEDx YouTube channel and the TEDx website, allowing a global audience to access the inspiring content..

Can anyone organize a TEDx event?

Yes, individuals or groups can apply to organize a TEDx event in their community. However, they must adhere to TEDx guidelines and standards to ensure quality and consistency..

What makes a TEDx talk different from a TED talk?

TEDx talks are locally organized and independently run, while TED talks are curated by the main TED organization. Both platforms share the goal of spreading ideas but operate on different scales and structures..
