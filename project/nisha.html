Nisha Sarangh

Nisha Sarangh is a prominent figure in the Malayalam entertainment industry.

Film and Television Journey

Nisha Sarangh has carved a niche for herself in Malayalam cinema. While the exact year of her film debut remains elusive, she has played memorable roles in movies like "Drishyam" and "Agnisakshi". Her filmography likely extends beyond these two titles.

Television Stardom and Accolades

Nisha Sarangh has made a significant mark on Malayalam television. Her comedic talent shone through in the popular sitcom "Uppum Mulakum", where her portrayal of Neelima earned her the prestigious Kerala State Television Award for Best Comedian in 2017. This award is a testament to her ability to make audiences laugh.

Beyond the Screen

Nisha Sarangh's talents extend far beyond acting. She is also a skilled classical dancer, showcasing her grace and artistry on stage. Additionally, her voice lends itself to singing, further enriching her artistic repertoire.
